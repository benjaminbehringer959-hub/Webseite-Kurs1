## Tag 1 -
**Task 1:** Formulate a personal SMART goal for your social media use.
* Example:* "On school days I use social media for a maximum of 30 minutes per day."
**Task 2:** Start your course journal (date, goal, motivation).
* Example:* "I am doing the course because I often get distracted while studying."
**Task 3:** Do a short focus test (5-10 minutes).
* Example:* Mark multiples of 3 on a number sheet for 2 minutes.
**Bonus:** Screenshot or roughly estimate your screen time.
* Example:* "Today about 2 hours of social media."

## Tag 2 -
**Task 1:** Turn off all social media notifications for 24 hours.
* Example:* Mute Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat completely.
**Task 2:** Note every urge to check anyway.
* Example:* "3:30 pm - boredom during the break."
**Bonus:** Short exchange with one person.
* Example:* "I noticed how automatic grabbing my phone is."

## Tag 3 -
**Task 1:** Log at least 6 social media sessions today.
* Example:* "08:10-08:15 Instagram."
**Task 2:** Mark each session as active or passive.
* Example:* Active = writing a message, passive = scrolling.
**Task 3:** Do a completely phone-free study phase.
* Example:* 45 minutes studying, phone in another room.
**Bonus:** Time your study phase and note it.
* Example:* "45 minutes without interruption."

## Tag 4 -
**Task 1:** Write down the trigger before each opening.
* Example:* "Stress after homework."
**Task 2:** Identify your three most frequent triggers.
* Example:* Boredom, stress, waiting.
**Task 3:** Choose a replacement action for each trigger.
* Example:* When bored -> stand up and move for 2 minutes.
**Bonus:** Avoid one trigger on purpose.
* Example:* Do not take your phone to bed.

## Tag 5 -
**Task 1:** Disable all social media notifications.
* Example:* No push messages from TikTok or Instagram.
**Task 2:** Turn off notification previews.
* Example:* No message previews on the lock screen.
**Task 3:** Write a short observation in the evening.
* Example:* "I was interrupted less while studying."
**Bonus:** Time-limit messaging apps.
* Example:* Open WhatsApp only at fixed times.

## Tag 6 -
**Task 1:** Move social apps into a folder or to a back page.
* Example:* Folder "Distractions" on page 3.
**Task 2:** Remove widgets and quick shortcuts.
* Example:* Delete the Instagram widget.
**Task 3:** Place study or productivity apps visibly.
* Example:* School app on the home screen.
**Bonus:** Take a before/after photo.
* Example:* Screenshot of the new home screen.

## Tag 7 -
**Task 1:** Create a list with at least six replacement actions.
* Example:* Stretch, drink water, step outside.
**Task 2:** Use a replacement action whenever the urge to scroll appears.
* Example:* Instead of scrolling -> 10 push-ups.
**Task 3:** Do at least six replacement actions today.
* Example:* Six intentional interruptions during the day.
**Bonus:** Mark the most effective replacement action.
* Example:* Movement helps most.

## Tag 8 -
**Task 1:** Set a fixed social media time window.
* Example:* 6:00-6:30 pm.
**Task 2:** Turn on app limits.
* Example:* Instagram max 30 minutes.
**Task 3:** Use a timer or the system feature.
* Example:* Screen time on the phone.
**Bonus:** Test a second short window.
* Example:* 10 minutes in the evening.

## Tag 9 -
**Task 1:** Enable grayscale for 24-48 hours.
* Example:* Turn off colors completely.
**Task 2:** Stay screen-free for 30 minutes in the evening.
* Example:* Read or listen to music.
**Task 3:** Choose a calm alternative on purpose.
* Example:* Stretch before sleep.
**Bonus:** Note your time of falling asleep.
* Example:* "Asleep at 10:45 pm."

## Tag 10 -
**Task 1:** Compare your use from day 1 and day 10.
* Example:* "On average 30 minutes less per day."
**Task 2:** Note three things that helped.
* Example:* Notifications off, clear study spot.
**Task 3:** Adjust your goal if needed.
* Example:* Make the goal more realistic.
**Bonus:** Write a short weekly reflection.
* Example:* "I study more calmly now."

## Tag 11 -
**Task 1:** Avoid social media for 20-30 minutes after waking.
* Example:* Set the alarm to 7:00, phone on the shelf, breakfast and a quick plan (7:00-7:20).
**Task 2:** Use the morning time for a short offline action.
* Example:* Write 3 school tasks (math exercises, vocab, presentation).
**Bonus:** Place the phone at night so you do not grab it immediately.
* Example:* Put the phone on the desk instead of the pillow.

## Tag 12 -
**Task 1:** Set up a fixed study place with no phone in sight.
* Example:* Desk with notebook, book, pen, water - phone in another room.
**Task 2:** Do two phone-free study phases there (e.g., 2x45 min or 3x25 min).
* Example:* 4:00-4:45 math (phone in backpack), 5:00-5:45 English.
**Task 3:** After each phase, briefly note if you were less distracted.
* Example:* "Fewer interruptions, tasks done faster."
**Bonus:** Mark the study place visibly (post-it "Study time").
* Example:* Post-it "phone out of sight" on the desk.

## Tag 13 -
**Task 1:** Plan fixed study blocks with clear times.
* Example:* 3:30-4:00 math, 4:10-4:40 English.
**Task 2:** Prepare everything you need before each block.
* Example:* Book, notebook, pen ready, close unnecessary tabs.
**Task 3:** Keep breaks phone-free (short movement outside).
* Example:* 5 minutes at the window, 2-minute walk, drink water.
**Bonus:** Start with the hardest subject in the first block.
* Example:* Physics first, easier vocab later.

## Tag 14 -
**Task 1:** Arrange short study times and rules with another person.
* Example:* "Mon/Tue/Thu 6:00-6:30 together, no social media."
**Task 2:** Send a short check-in after studying (1-2 sentences).
* Example:* "Did 2x25 min, felt productive."
**Task 3:** Write one simple shared rule (written or by message).
* Example:* "No Instagram during our study time."
**Bonus:** Set a small weekly goal (hours).
* Example:* "6 hours together this week."

## Tag 15 -
**Task 1:** Unfollow at least 8-10 accounts that only distract you.
* Example:* Unfollow celebrity and meme pages you only scroll.
**Task 2:** Create a favorites list with three useful sources.
* Example:* One learning YouTube, a uni newsfeed, and a podcast.
**Task 3:** Plan a fixed reading time (e.g., 20 minutes) for useful content.
* Example:* 8:00-8:20 pm reading instead of scrolling.
**Bonus:** Read 10-15 pages in a printed book today.
* Example:* A chapter in a textbook or nonfiction.

## Tag 16 -
**Task 1:** Choose a small routine after studying (e.g., stretching).
* Example:* After each study phase, 2 minutes of stretching.
**Task 2:** Do the routine at least twice today (after two study phases).
* Example:* After block 1 and block 2, 2 minutes each.
**Task 3:** Note briefly whether the routine helped you avoid scrolling.
* Example:* "After stretching I did not want to grab the phone."
**Bonus:** Tie the routine to a fixed time.
* Example:* After school, go outside for 5 minutes.

## Tag 17 -
**Task 1:** Write one sentence about what you do immediately if you notice you scrolled.
* Example:* "If I scroll: put phone away, breathe for 2 minutes, then continue."
**Task 2:** Practice this replacement once when the urge appears.
* Example:* Pause, drink water, breathe 2 minutes instead of scrolling.
**Task 3:** Note briefly how it helped you.
* Example:* "After breathing I could refocus."
**Bonus:** Inform your buddy briefly if you slipped.
* Example:* "Scrolled briefly, doing 20 min extra focus now."

## Tag 18 -
**Task 1:** Write down for three of your most-used apps what you truly want to use them for.
* Example:* WhatsApp = coordination; Instagram = project ideas max 20 min; YouTube = learning videos.
**Task 2:** Adjust app settings accordingly (push notifications off/limited).
* Example:* Instagram pushes off, WhatsApp notifications on for the group.
**Task 3:** Check in the evening if you followed the rules and note duration.
* Example:* "Instagram 15 minutes, only for research."
**Bonus:** Skip one app for 24 hours.
* Example:* No TikTok today.

## Tag 19 -
**Task 1:** Set a fixed time for your last social media use in the evening.
* Example:* Social media only until 9:30 pm today.
**Task 2:** Choose a fixed place where the phone goes after the last check.
* Example:* After 9:30 the phone is on the desk, not in bed.
**Task 3:** Plan a simple, calm evening activity without the phone.
* Example:* Read 10 minutes or listen to music.
**Bonus:** Notice when the evening "just scroll" impulse appears.
* Example:* It often comes right after homework.

## Tag 20 -
**Task 1:** Think of two stressful days and decide how social media should work on them.
* Example:* Exam day: social media after 7 pm, max 30 minutes; training day: only after training.
**Task 2:** Set fixed daily social media times for next week and write them down.
* Example:* Mon-Fri 6:30-7:00, Sat 10:00-10:30, Sun 7:00-7:30.
**Task 3:** Create a simple 7-day plan with time windows and a short weekly review (10 min).
* Example:* Plan daily 6:30-7:00 social, Sunday 7:30 review notes.
**Bonus:** Put the weekly review into your calendar.
* Example:* "Sunday 7:30 - social media review (10 min)."
