Day 10

Day 10

When you think back on the last days, some things may feel clearer than at the beginning. Maybe you did not constantly do something "different," but you noticed more. Maybe also that quiet feeling: something is not quite the same as before, even if you cannot name it. That is exactly what today is about.

Today is not a conclusion and not a final verdict. It is a quiet look back at what has shown up, without evaluation and without expectations. You take time to compare how you assessed yourself at the start and how it feels now. You also repeat the short focus test and look at your goal again. Not to check or measure, but to gain orientation.

The goal today is not a balance sheet, but to understand what this first week has done for you.

Tasks for today

Task 1

Compare your use from day 1 and day 10. * Example:* "On average 30 minutes less per day."

Task 2

Note three things that helped. * Example:* Notifications off, clear study spot.

Task 3

Adjust your goal if needed. * Example:* Make the goal more realistic.

Bonus

Write a short weekly reflection. * Example:* "I study more calmly now."

Fact of the day: People often recognize change only through comparison.

Why this works

Change is hard to notice in the moment. Comparison makes it visible. Looking back at day 1 helps you see what shifted and what still needs support.

Goals are not fixed forever. Adjusting a goal to fit your real life makes it more usable, not weaker.
